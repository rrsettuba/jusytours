Our Story

Who We Are

Jusy Tours and Travel is Uganda's premier safari company, founded in 2010 with a passion for showcasing East Africa's breathtaking wildlife and rich cultural heritage. What began as a small family business has grown into a respected tour operator known for exceptional service and unforgettable experiences.

Our team of expert guides and travel consultants brings together decades of local knowledge with international hospitality standards. We're proud to be 100% Ugandan-owned, offering authentic experiences that directly benefit local communities.

From our headquarters in Kampala to our network of partners across Uganda and Rwanda, every member of the Jusy Tours family shares our commitment to sustainable tourism and personalized service.

Our Mission

To create transformative travel experiences that connect visitors with Uganda's natural wonders while supporting conservation efforts and local communities.

We believe responsible tourism should:

  • Educate visitors about wildlife conservation
  • Create sustainable livelihoods for local communities
  • Preserve cultural heritage
  • Minimize environmental impact

Every safari we organize contributes to these goals through partnerships with national parks, community projects, and eco-friendly lodges.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best time to book a safari?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for peak seasons (June-September and December-February). For last-minute bookings, we'll do our best to accommodate you based on availability.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, credit cards (Visa/Mastercard), and mobile money payments. A 30% deposit is required to secure your booking.

Do you offer customized safari packages?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating personalized safari experiences tailored to your interests, budget, and travel style. Just let us know your preferences.

What safety measures do you have in place?

Your safety is our priority. All our vehicles are well-maintained, guides are first-aid certified, and we follow all national park safety protocols. We also provide 24/7 emergency support.

What should I pack for my Uganda safari?

We'll send you a detailed packing list after booking, but essentials include neutral-colored clothing, comfortable walking shoes, binoculars, sunscreen, and a good camera.
